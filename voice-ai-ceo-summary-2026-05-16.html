§02 What the Memo Gets Wrong

Five things to fix before we decide.

The memo's headline numbers are persuasive but the underlying assumptions don't survive contact with our actual 2026 OKRs, our team realities, and how the Conduit and ElevenLabs products actually price.

  1. It's optimised for 500 listings. We're targeting 180 in 2026.
    All the dramatic savings tables ($245k vs Conduit, $140k vs hiring) are at 500 listings — 2028–2029 territory. Our 2026 OKR is 180 units. Re-run at the real target and the gaps shrink dramatically.
  2. "Same 3 people forever" doesn't hold.
    Even with 70% AI deflection, absolute human-handled volume at 500 listings is 3.6× today. Real minimum is 4–5 Colombia headcount at scale, not 3. Adds $12–24k/yr to every "with AI" scenario.
  3. Conduit's scale cost is overstated.
    Re-running the Salesbricks math at 500 listings: ~$96k/yr, not $110k. At 300: ~$61k, not $66k. Small, but it inflates the "savings vs Conduit" story.
  4. ElevenLabs is mis-framed as "raw voice."
    The pricing cited is ElevenLabs Agents — a managed conversational AI product (TTS + STT + LLM orchestration + telephony). Not "raw ingredients." Build effort is roughly half what the memo implies — closer to 80–150 hours on top of the Mews connector I'm already building.
  5. Two big things missing: AOS strategy + legal regime.
    Voice AI built on AOS becomes Nestral-productizable IP — directly aligned with OKR #2. Buying Conduit doesn't. And the memo treats Collection (VV, only needs reachable contact) and Hotels (boutique-hotel category, likely needs staffed reception) identically. They are not.

§05 Why This Is About More Than Cost

Three angles the memo doesn't surface.

A · AOS
Voice AI = AOS IP
OKR #2 is productizing the Airnest Operating System. Voice AI built on Mews + AOS is exactly the kind of module Nestral Solutions can sell to other operators. Conduit gives us none of this asset.
B · Legal
Collection ≠ Hotels
Canarias Decreto 113/2015 only requires a reachable 24/7 contact for VV — one person is enough. Boutique-hotel category under regional turismo law likely requires staffed reception. Any "autonomous" plan must respect this split.
C · Bandwidth
Mews connector first
My CEO-agreed primary work is the Mews API Connector (customers.py next). It's the foundation any DIY voice AI calls into. Pulling me onto voice AI in 2026 puts the connector at risk and delays everything downstream.
