§01 Where We Are

Poem is configured. This call clears the runway for the rest.

Purpose of the call
Hotel Masanet's full configuration is now replicated onto Edificio Poem — accounting, policies, rate groups, rates, age categories, products. Poem is the first go-live, w/c 25 May, then roughly one property per week through early July. This call is to clear the go-live blockers and confirm the open configuration points before we repeat the process across the remaining five properties.

Replicated to Poem so far (Masanet as source of truth): Accounting Configuration (general settings, the 23 payment/charge → category mappings, billing management), Enterprise/property settings, the Stay service landing config, 5 age categories, the Cama Extra product, both cancellation and both payment policies, 4 rate groups, and all 10 rates. What is not done — and what this brief is about — is everything that needs a Mews answer, a commercial decision, or is currently blocked.

Roll-out queue after Poem: Airnest Portfolio → Bandama Golf Hotel → Arena & Cielo → Eleven Boutique Apartments → Hotel Emete & Coworking. Whatever Mews tells us today, we apply six times — so it is worth getting precise answers.
§02 Blockers — Go-Live Critical

Two things that can stop or compromise the cutover.

B1 Mews Payments not provisioned on Poem Blocker

While replicating Masanet's Accounting Configuration → General settings, every field copied except the Invoice payment methods chip-selector — it does not render on Poem's form at all. Masanet has Payment card enabled there. The likely cause is that Mews Payments is not yet provisioned for Poem (still in Settings/Onboarding). Without it, card payments and payment-request links won't work at cutover.

Ask: What is the trigger for the Invoice payment methods field to appear, and what's the provisioning path + lead time to enable Mews Payments on Poem and the other five properties (go-live w/c 1 Jun → 6 Jul)?
B2 Rate pricing — no source of truth yet Blocker

The 10 rates exist on Poem but carry no base prices. Mews onboarding flags "Set up bookable rates" as Required and lists every rate as outdated. This is 10 rates × 6 properties of €/night pricing — a commercial decision, not a template copy.

Ask: What does Mews expect as the source of pricing — imported from current Guesty rates, driven by Atomize RMS, or manual entry by revenue management? And does "Select tax for all base rates" (IGIC 7%) get handled in the same pricing workflow?
§03 Configuration Questions

Lower-stakes clarifications — confirm before we repeat ×6.

Q1 "Flexible" cancellation policy semantics Question

Both Masanet and Poem have a policy named Flexible set to: 100% fee / deadline 1d after check-in time / fee extent Nights and products. Functionally a guest is fully refundable until one day past check-in, then charged 100%. "Flexible" normally implies a window before check-in.

Ask: Is "1d after check-in" the intended Airnest configuration, or should it be 1d before check-in time to match the standard Booking.com / Airbnb "Flexible" semantics?
Q2 Rate primary-name locale stickiness Question

Rates created during onboarding (when the property default culture was en-US) keep their original English name even after the default culture is changed to es-ES and a new names.es-ES value is set. On Poem the renamed "Fully Flexible OTA" rate still displays as "Fully Flexible" in the rate list, the base-rate dropdown, and dependent rates' base-rate column. Short name and external name update correctly.

Ask: How do we change the primary stored name so it displays consistently across the UI and to the OTAs?
§04 Setup Items Needing Mews Input

Things we can't replicate until we know what Masanet has.

S1 Per-room mapping for SES.Hospedajes police registration Question

Spanish police accommodation registration requires a registration code per room/space. We need a per-room mapping so guest registration data submits correctly.

Ask: How is the per-room police-registration mapping configured in Mews, and where do the per-room codes live?
S2 INE occupancy export — registration number Question

Draft config: name INE occupancy export integration, service Stay, tax identifier TBD, registration number TBD. The registration number is the blocker.

Ask: What registration number does the INE occupancy export require, and where do we obtain it — Mews-side or from the Spanish statistics authority?
S3 Room access-code storage for check-in emails Question

Automated check-in emails need a per-room access code. We have not decided where these live.

Ask: Does Mews support a per-room custom field for access codes that can be merged into check-in email templates, or should codes live in an external store / Airnest tool?
§05 Things To Raise — Proactive

Not yet tracked, but worth surfacing while we have Kenya.

P1 Is there a "clone property configuration" feature?

We are manually replicating Masanet's configuration onto six properties, section by section. If Mews has a native copy/clone-settings feature, it would save days of work and remove the human-error risk of hand-copying.

Ask: Does Mews support cloning a property's configuration (accounting, policies, rate groups, rates, products) to another property?
P2 OTA channel cutover sequencing

When Booking.com / Airbnb / Expedia connections move from Guesty to Mews, there is a window where both PMSs could be live on the same channel.

Ask: What's the recommended sequence for switching each OTA connection, and how do we avoid double-bookings or availability gaps during the switch?
P3 The onboarding "final countdown" — 5 of 10

Masanet's onboarding dashboard shows a "final countdown" of 5/10 tasks done.

Ask: Which of those 10 tasks are hard go-live blockers versus things that can be completed after launch?
P4 Connector API for production data migration

We plan to migrate guest/reservation data via the Mews Connector API. We need production credentials and to know the Editable History Window per property (how far back financial/reservation data can be written).

Ask: How do we get production Connector API credentials and scopes, and how is the Editable History Window set per property?
P5 Is one property per week realistic?

Our committed cadence is one property go-live per week from w/c 25 May. The blockers above (Mews Payments lead time, pricing decisions) could threaten it.

Ask: Given the blockers, does the Mews onboarding team consider one-per-week realistic? What's the critical-path item per property?
